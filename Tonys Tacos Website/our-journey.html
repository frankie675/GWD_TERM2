OUR PASSION

Welcome to Tony's Tacos! We are a beloved food truck based in Bristol Broadmead, serving up delicious, authentic Mexican cuisine since 2018.
Our story began when Tony, our founder and head chef, decided to bring the flavors of his childhood to the streets of Bristol. With a passion for cooking and a dedication to quality ingredients, Tony's Tacos quickly became a local favorite.
At Tony's Tacos, we believe that food is meant to be shared and enjoyed with others. That's why we take great care in preparing our dishes, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods. From our mouth-watering tacos to our flavorful burritos and quesadillas, every bite is a taste of Mexico that will transport your taste buds.

OUR VALUES

But it's not just about the food - it's also about the experience. Our friendly and welcoming staff are always ready to greet you with a smile and make your visit to Tony's Tacos a memorable one.
We are committed to bringing our customers the best possible food and service, and we are grateful for the support of our community. Thank you for choosing Tony's Tacos - we look forward to serving you soon!
